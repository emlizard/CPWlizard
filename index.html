Formulas Used

Effective Width:

\[ W_{\text{eff}} = \begin{cases} W, & t \le 0 \\ W + \dfrac{t}{\pi} \ln\left(1 + \dfrac{4 e h}{t} \right), & t > 0 \end{cases} \]

Elliptic Modulus:

\[ k = \frac{W_{\text{eff}}}{W_{\text{eff}} + 2S}, \quad k' = \sqrt{1 - k^2} \]

CPW:

\[ \epsilon_{\text{eff}} = \epsilon_{\text{eff,0}} + (\epsilon_r - \epsilon_{\text{eff,0}}) \left(1 - e^{-f/f_p} \right) \quad\text{and}\quad Z_0 = \frac{30\pi}{\sqrt{\epsilon_{\text{eff}}}} \cdot \frac{K(k')}{K(k)} \]

CPWG:

\[ \epsilon_{\text{eff}} = \epsilon_r - 0.5 + 0.5\left(1 - e^{-f/f_p} \right), \quad Z_0 = \frac{30\pi}{\sqrt{\epsilon_{\text{eff}}}} \cdot \frac{K(k')}{K(k)} \]
